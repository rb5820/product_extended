Product Extended

Enhance Your Product Management

Comprehensive tools for managing product details across multiple companies and capturing detailed manufacturer information.

This module extends the standard Odoo product module with additional features for multi-company environments and manufacturer details.

Key Features

  • Multi-Company Support: Associate products with multiple companies for better visibility across your organization.
  • Manufacturer Details: Track comprehensive manufacturer information including part numbers and model numbers.
  • Supplier Information: Record specific supplier details for each product.
  • Physical Specifications: Manage product dimensions (length, width, height) and weight.
  • Country of Origin: Track where products are manufactured for compliance and reporting.
  • Advanced Filtering: Search and filter products by manufacturer, supplier, or dimensions.
  • Integration: Seamlessly integrates with existing Odoo product and inventory modules.

Installation

To install the Product Extended module, follow these steps:

  1. Download the module files from the repository.
  2. Copy the module folder to your Odoo addons directory.
  3. Update the Odoo app list by going to Apps > Update Apps List.
  4. Search for "Product Extended" in the apps list.
  5. Click "Install" to install the module.

Usage

Once installed, you can access the extended product features from the Inventory module. The module allows you to:

  • Manage manufacturer details for each product.
  • Track physical specifications like dimensions and weight.
  • Associate products with multiple companies in multi-company environments.
  • Search and filter products by manufacturer, supplier, or country of origin.
  • Group products by relevant attributes for better analysis.
  • Manage detailed supplier information for procurement.

Multi-Company Configuration

To set up multi-company product visibility:

  1. Go to the product form view
  2. Find the "Allowed Companies" field below the barcode
  3. Add the companies that should have access to this product
  4. Optionally, use the "Disallowed Companies" field to explicitly exclude companies
  5. Products with no allowed companies assigned are visible to all companies (unless explicitly disallowed)
  6. A company cannot be both allowed and disallowed (validation will prevent this)

Manufacturer Details

To add manufacturer information:

  1. Open the product form
  2. Navigate to the "Manufacturing Details" tab
  3. Fill in manufacturer, part numbers, and physical specifications

Views Added

  • Manufacturing Details tab in product form
  • Multi-company fields in product form
  • Enhanced search filters for manufacturer and supplier
  • Group by options for manufacturer, supplier, and country
